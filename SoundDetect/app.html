Welcome to HubSpy!

This system will help Passerelles Numeriques Philippines educators to monitor who are those specified rooms who are creating noise and who are very noisy. The system would detect if the scholars who are staying in a certain room are very noisy. If the system detects that a certain room is quite noisy, the system will give an alert to the educator that the scholars who are staying in that room are creating noise. And by that, educators would have the chance to correct, reprimand or worse give a penalty for certain room who are reported by the system to be noisy. The educators would be continually notified by the system if the scholars who are staying in a specific room are really very noisy. The system would just stop sending notifications to the educators if the scholars are already minimizing their voice or if they are already quiet and serene and the educators have clicked "OK" to really assure she/he has identified or get the noise. This system is developed by the Group 4 Team Army as final project for their SD Elective 2 course with the help of their instructor, Sir Junrey Ansing. The group members are proudly from PNP Batch 2020B.
